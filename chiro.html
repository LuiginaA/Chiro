Vision Statement

I, Dr Janique Belli, believe everyone deserves the highest quality of a pain free healthy life. My philosophy is to offer my patients the very best optimal personalised care, by achieving and maintaining a healthy life accompanied by impeccable service in a comfortable environment. My goal is that my patients leave my office empowered with a better understanding of the chiropractic treatment they received and a greater knowledge of the functioning of their body. Chiropractic is not merely a clinical procedure, it is a healing discipline of philosophy, science, and art that is concerned with each patient as a whole.

Mission Statement

With passion and compassion, “My Mission” is to:

  • Each patient is considered a unique individual with specific health wants and needs.
  • Treatments are based on a holistic approach with the spirit of caring, taking the entire person into account and not just their symptoms.
  • To help people find relief from pain and poor health eliminating unnecessary suffering.
  • My goal is not only to alleviate pain, but more importantly, to inspire and educate patients to become active participants in their own well being Identifying and correcting the underlying causes of dis-ease and injury.
  • To offer a constructive influence in the lives, I have the privilege to interact with my patients, by changing their perception and helping them to live well, younger-longer.
  • Honouring the innate healing capabilities of every patient regardless of their condition.
  • Most importantly I endeavour to be the Chiropractic Wellness Centre and recognized leader in health care quality.

With this mission, I strive to help others by chiropractic and wellness care achieving health, happiness, and fulfilment through a positive and mental attitude, patient education, and chiropractic care, maintaining the highest ethics, integrity, and professional standards—never ceasing to improve my services at every level, today, tomorrow, and for generations to come. Enabling patients with the information and inspiration needed to choose optimal health solutions for themselves and their families.
